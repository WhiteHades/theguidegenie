# Privacy Policy

The Guide Genie processes the information needed to run a tour marketplace, confirm bookings, support guides, and keep the platform secure.

## What we collect

- account details such as name, email address, and optional phone number
- guide profile details such as biography, city, contact email, and avatar
- booking details such as party size, guest contact details, booking status, and timestamps
- technical data needed for security, abuse prevention, and service reliability

## Why we use it

- create and manage traveller and guide accounts
- publish tours and upcoming availability
- confirm, manage, or cancel bookings
- prevent abuse, fraud, and unauthorised access
- improve reliability, performance, and product decisions

## Who can see it

- travellers can access their own account and booking data
- guides can access the bookings and tour data tied to their own listings
- authorised infrastructure providers may process data on our behalf to host the app, database, authentication, and media storage

## Booking links

Guest bookings may include a private management link so travellers can review or cancel a reservation without creating an account. Treat that link like a password and only share it with people who should manage the booking.

## Retention

We keep data for as long as it is needed to provide the service, resolve disputes, meet legal obligations, and maintain basic audit and security records.

## Your choices

You can update profile details inside the app, request account changes through the available support channel, and stop using the platform at any time.

## Security

We use authentication controls, database access rules, and server-side validation to reduce unauthorised access. No internet service can be guaranteed to be perfectly secure, so keep your account credentials and private booking links confidential.

## Updates

We may revise this policy as the product changes. Material updates take effect when the revised policy is published on this page.
